Help & Guidelines

Guide for new staff members

Welcome to Ocean View Resort System

This system helps you manage all room reservations, guest records, and billing for Ocean View Resort, Galle. Below are guidelines for each feature.

🔐 User Authentication (Login)
Use your assigned username and password to log in. Never share your credentials. Admin accounts can manage staff. Receptionist accounts handle reservations and billing only. Contact your manager if you forget your password.
📋 Adding a New Reservation
Navigate to New Reservation in the sidebar. Fill in all required fields marked with (*): Guest Name, Contact Number, Room Type, Check-In, and Check-Out dates. The system will auto-generate a unique Reservation Number (e.g. R-00001). Select the appropriate room type and available room. The estimated cost will appear automatically. Click Confirm Reservation to save.
🔍 Viewing Reservation Details
Go to the Reservations section to see all bookings. Use the search bar to filter by guest name or reservation number. Use the status filter to view specific booking statuses. Click the View button on any row to see full reservation details in a popup.
💰 Calculating & Printing a Bill
Go to the Billing section and enter the Reservation Number. The system will automatically calculate the bill based on number of nights × room rate + 10% tax. Click Print Bill to open a printable version. After payment is collected, click Mark as Paid to update the reservation status.
📊 Overview Dashboard
The Overview page shows a summary of current reservations, including total bookings, confirmed, checked-in counts, and estimated revenue. Recent reservations are shown in the table for quick access.
🚪 Exiting the System
Always click Sign Out at the bottom of the sidebar before leaving your workstation. This protects guest data and prevents unauthorized access. Never close the browser tab without signing out properly.
